About GHOST

Our mission to secure digital identities in an era of synthetic media

The Challenge

In today's digital landscape, synthetic media (deepfakes and voice clones) pose an unprecedented threat to identity verification systems. Traditional methods are increasingly vulnerable to these sophisticated attacks.

Problem Statement

Current identity verification systems fail to detect AI-generated synthetic media, leading to increased fraud and security breaches.

Our Solution

GHOST combines multiple AI models to detect subtle artifacts in both visual and auditory media that indicate synthetic manipulation.

Innovation

Our multi-modal approach analyzes both facial micro-expressions and voice spectral patterns simultaneously for unprecedented accuracy.
