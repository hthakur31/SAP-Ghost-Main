Best Practices for Optimal Results

Recommended

  • Use audio samples of 3+ seconds
  • Ensure clear speech with minimal background noise
  • Use high-quality recordings (44.1kHz or higher)
  • Test multiple samples for verification

Avoid

  • Very short audio clips (<2 seconds)
  • Heavy background noise or music
  • Heavily compressed or low-quality audio
  • Multiple speakers in the same file
